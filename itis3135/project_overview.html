Page Design

Page Name: Home

Purpose: To highlight some of the photographer's best one shot pictures to attract potential clients.

Audience/Users: Potential clients looking for photography services.

Content: A few best photos, description of Triad Photography Group and short introduction.

User Input?: No.

Validations: No.

Interactive Elements: Links.

Actions: Navigate to other pages.

Special Notes: N/A.



Page Name: Portfolio

Purpose: To showcase a variety of photography work to demonstrate skills and style.

Audience/Users: Potential clients interested in viewing the photographer's work.

Content: Galleries of photos categorized by type (e.g., weddings, portraits, events).

User Input?: No.

Validations: No.

Interactive Elements: Image galleries with search category feature.

Actions: View photos in larger format in another tab.

Special Notes: N/A.



Page Name: Contact

Purpose: To provide a way for potential clients to reach out for inquiries or bookings.

Audience/Users: Potential clients looking to contact the photographer.

Content: Contact form with fields for name, email, phone number, and message.

User Input?: Yes.

Validations: Required fields for name, email, and message; email format validation.

Interactive Elements: Contact form.

Actions: Submit inquiry via email.

Special Notes: N/A.



Page Name: About

Purpose: To provide background information about the photographer and their services.

Audience/Users: Potential clients wanting to learn more about the photographer.

Content: Biography, photography philosophy, and details about gallery.

User Input?: No.

Validations: No.

Interactive Elements: Links to social media profiles

Actions: Navigate to social media pages.

Special Notes: N/A.



Page Name: Services/FAQ

Purpose: To outline the photography services offered, pricing information, and common questions.

Audience/Users: Potential clients interested in hiring the photographer and the prices and packages offered.

User Input?: No.

Validations: No.
