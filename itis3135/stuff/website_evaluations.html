Website Evaluations

Stripe

Stripe is an online payment platform with a clean and professional homepage that demonstrates how to follow CRAP design principles effectively.

  • Contrast: Dark text on light backgrounds and bright accent colors make the website clear.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts, buttons, and spacing help establish a sense of unity throughout the site.
  • Alignment: Everything is grid based, aligned neatly, giving it a polished and balanced look.
  • Proximity: Related information such as the feature lists are grouped well, reducing clutter and helping users find what they need.

Pacific Northwest X-Ray

Pacific Northwest X-Ray is a medical supplier website that has a cluttered design with too many competing elements and colors, making it hard to focus on important information.

  • Contrast: Overdone bright, flashing colors compete instead of guiding the eye.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent design elements clash rather than reinforce a theme.
  • Alignment: Poor text, images, and banners feel scattered with no clear grid.
  • Proximity: It is too much packed together with little spacing, making it hard to read and separate each section.
